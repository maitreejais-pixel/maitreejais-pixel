# 💫 About Me:
“Think. Innovate. Impact.”<br>Maitree Jaiswal is an Undergraduate Student, driven by curiosity and passion for Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science, Maitree has explored cutting-edge research during her internship at IIT Delhi, focusing on Brain Mapping-Neuroscience and Healthcare Technology. She has also worked on cybersecurity-driven web application development and vulnerability assessment at DRDO, and developed secure, location-aware mobile applications with robust backend integration for the Airports Authority of India (AAI).<br>Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, C/C++, Java, PHP, React Native, Node.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Docker, RESTful APIs, and Machine Learning fundamentals, she thrives on developing impactful projects, leveraging her coding expertise to turn innovative ideas into reality. A staunch advocate for women in tech, she aspires to make technology more inclusive and transformative.<br>She is a quick learner, who adapts, learns and unlearns constantly, she loves exploring and trying hands at new technologies.<br>Maitree welcomes opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals and organizations committed to harnessing technology for a better future.
